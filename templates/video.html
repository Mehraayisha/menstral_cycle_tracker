Understanding Your Menstrual Cycle 📅

Your menstrual cycle is typically 28 days, but it can vary from 21 to 35 days for many individuals. 🗓️It’s essential to track your cycle to understand your fertility window and overall health.💫Understanding each phases of the cycle can help you prepare for symptoms like cramps, mood swings, or energy dips. 🌟
